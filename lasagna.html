Chicken Taco Lasagna

A chicken taco twist on the classic lasagna.

Cook: 1 hr 15 mins

Toal: 1 hr 35 mins

Prep: 20 mins

Servings: 8

Yield: 1 9x9-inch lasagna

Ingredients

  1. 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4-inch strips
  2. ½ cup lime juice
  3. 2 (1 ounce) packets taco seasoning mix (such as Old El Paso®)
  4. 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  5. 1 cup sour cream
  6. 1 cup salsa
  7. salt and ground black pepper to taste
  8. 1 (8 ounce) package no-boil lasagna noodles, or to taste
